our services

Whether you're in training, returning to GP, or switching careers, we'll provide support for every shift you work.

Options designed just for you

Doctors have different preferences, and so do we. Whether you want short telehealth shifts after hours, longer 8-hour telehealth blocks, or prefer us to handle transport and equipment while others bring their own – we're totally flexible and here to support your needs.

Our services are billed

We charge a gap fee, which means you earn more. With plenty of patients willing to pay for quality telehealth and home visits, you can earn double or even triple per patient with Doctor Spharm compared to bulk-billing services.

Healthcare compliance

While some services still use outdated healthcare processes that have rightly caused concern, our app streamlines compliance. It lets you triage your own patients, document thoroughly, and choose the correct healthcare codes easily and efficiently.

Control of your shift

For each shift, you decide whether to do home visits, telehealth, or both, your start and end times, the number of patients you'll see, and the areas you'll cover. You can even adjust these during your shift, so you're always in control and finish on time.

Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of work times are available at Doctor Spharm?

Doctor Spharm provides a range of flexible options, from in-person home care to remote telehealth sessions, or a mix of both. You can select daytime, evening, or night shifts across various locations.

How much could I earn with Doctor Spharm?

Boost your income significantly compared to standard services! Earn up to ₹14,000 for each home visit and ₹8,000 per telehealth appointment. Our private billing approach typically doubles or triples what you'd make with bulk billing.

What kind of support is offered during my work?

You're never on your own. Our experienced senior GPs are available 24/7 via phone for support. Plus, we can help with necessary equipment, transportation, and drivers, depending on what you need.

Can I manage my own work schedule?

Absolutely! Our user-friendly platform lets you decide your preferred shift times, the number of patients you see, the areas you want to cover, and whether you'd like to do home visits, telehealth, or both.

What qualifications do I need to join?

You'll need a current medical license. We welcome both seasoned GPs and those who are newer to after-hours care, and we offer training if you need it.

How does the payment process work?

Our compliant private billing system, which includes gap fees, ensures better earnings without any complicated upcoding. You have complete control over your billing decisions.
