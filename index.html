our services

Whether you're in training, returning to GP, or switching careers, we'll provide support for every shift you work.

Options designed just for you

Doctors have different preferences, and so do we. Whether you want short telehealth shifts after hours, longer 8-hour telehealth blocks, or prefer us to handle transport and equipment while others bring their own – we're totally flexible and here to support your needs.

Our services are billed

We charge a gap fee, which means you earn more. With plenty of patients willing to pay for quality telehealth and home visits, you can earn double or even triple per patient with Many Doctors compared to bulk-billing services.

Healthcare compliance

While some services still use outdated healthcare processes that have rightly caused concern, our app streamlines compliance. It lets you triage your own patients, document thoroughly, and choose the correct healthcare codes easily and efficiently.

Control of your shift

For each shift, you decide whether to do home visits, telehealth, or both, your start and end times, the number of patients you'll see, and the areas you'll cover. You can even adjust these during your shift, so you're always in control and finish on time.
